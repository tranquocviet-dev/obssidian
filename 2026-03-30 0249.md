Today I logged on Obsidian, and to my absolute surprise everything look so small I couldnt even read most of the things on the screen. That is a sign for me to change things out, so i bring out the big guns and scaled the text up. Now my screen is readable again.

Now it makes me wonder, is a screen with text so large the content is reduced per line better than a screen with less lines but more text each line? the answer is the one that you can see the text wins.

It makes me appreciate more and more [[Style is consistent constraint]], the more I read my own creations the more I realize just how having a simple consistent style changes my life.
