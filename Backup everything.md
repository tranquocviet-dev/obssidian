I have lost a lot of small things in my life. I have lost even more stupid things in my life. It has taught me how everything is not bound to me or to anyone for that matter, and I should back up everything i feel attatchment to.

Text files are the perfect example of this. Tons and tons of my notes are scattered around the place with zero way of getting back to, and I have lost ways of recollecting them. But once I built a solid backup foundation, I never had to worry about me losing them, instead I always have this safety that everything I care about will be stored somewhere, waiting for me to retrieve.

Plans are perhaps even more important to back up, because they contribute directly to your life experience and enjoyment. Making backup plans allow you to be flexible, and [[Flexibility is a virtue]].

Life is like that, if you care about something, make backups of them, and spare yourself the hassle of having to worry or be careful every time you try new things. Once you have a back up, be it note or plans, you will be able to enjoy life to its fullest.
