Flexibility is a paradox. When one is too flexible, they either do not have complete mastery over one of the subjects, or we like to think so. In reality this simply opens up more options for further development, while most people simply look at others' flexibility and downplay the individual strength of a person in each subject.

Flexibility is a superpower, for one can go from one subject and work to another in no time, opening up various ways for them to work. Being flexible creates a huge advantage over others, being able to fit in multiple positions, not being bound to any one.
