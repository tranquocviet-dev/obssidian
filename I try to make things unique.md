Uniqueness do not have to come in the context of an entire world. Even I am not confident that the world will have no one like me, though I do think I am quite unique myself. Nevertheless, I try my best to align with the rest if need be.

Being unique does not have to do with conforming to expectations. It just means that one must be willing to walk his own path and live his own life, against the scrutiny of the world.

That I am willing to do. Because [[Uniqueness is a paradox]].
