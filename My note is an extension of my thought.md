I used to think to take notes. Everything in my life only served to be part of my experience and fuel for the neverending notes, which grew tiresome fast. I was too conservative with my thoughts, my subjects, my ideas. It was time to change.

Then I started taking notes to think, for the first time. It was the right way to go, but by the time I realize it, my notes has devolved into something out of my own control. It has become the extension of my lessons, not my mind.

So i started taking notes to think again, now with resolve to make everything I write a part of my own understanding. Using someone else's words and lessson meant that I could not modify them to my own understanding. No, they have to come from me. I must be the one to write my own destiny, my own lessons. It is only me who is responsible for my mistakes, and therefore, only I am responsible for my own thoughts.
