Relationships (not to be confused with real life ones) are a study of the linking between aspects, for example [[Sets]] or [[Numbers]]. We try to establish relationships between such elements using arrows, similar to [[SQL]], and through that relationship we try ways to classify connections.

Relationships are flexible. A set can link to a number, or another set. All that matters is where the arrow head points at. This simplicity is appreciated, but sometimes requires careful observation in order not to be mistook for something else (see [[Simplicity does not mean good]])

Relationships are like sql, as we recycle the same types everywhere. One to one, One to Many, all of them are recycled relationship forms.
