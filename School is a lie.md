School is a lie. It's evil.

I'm not saying that school is useless, it obviously isn't, but the way this entity we're calling the "education system" works is not in favour of the learners. What good there is to teach students that they should follow an exact, singular method for everything? Or teach them to memorize things instead of truly understand concepts? Why trying to fit them into pre-determined shells and minds?

If life isn't perfect, it's the imperfections that make it beautiful, that make each of us unique. What "rights" do they have to take that away from the students?

Don't they dare.
