Sets are simple in that its structure do not possess any innate functions. Everything sets can store are stored in a "void" of a [[Collection]], meaning there is bound to be less constraint in this storage method.

However, [[Simplicity does not mean good]], and in the case of sets, simplicity means that there is not much you can do with a simple set. In the case of [[Matrix]], you have two dimensions of storage and a more-than-enough positioning system to index its entries, leading to it less than ideal for the vast majority of advanced use cases.
