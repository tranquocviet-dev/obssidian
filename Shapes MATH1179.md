Geometry, shapes, most things in life, are constructed using angles. Angles are constructed using [[Numbers MATH1179]]. The more you think about it, the more that Shapes and Numbers are one and the same. They may exist in different dimensions, different use cases, but they are fundamentally connected. So, [[Shapes are just math]].

Shapes can represent many things, even intangible things, in this world. Therefore, we can assume the understanding of shapes to be simple, which, [[Simplicity needs thoughts]]. It is our understanding of shapes that we need to hone, in order to get more proficient with our way when using shapes.
