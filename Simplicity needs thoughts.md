I once thought that Simplicity means less things to think about, and I was right, but I didnt balance it out by [[Thinking more about things that I already know]], either. In actuality, you dont think less by having less things to think about, but instead have to focus on what you already have instead.

(Part of an argument in [[Simplicity does not mean good]])
