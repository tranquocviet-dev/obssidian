It is often said that doing something by oneself can be very relaxing, and the experience is bound to produce much food for thought. The latter I agree wholeheartedly, not so much the former. It takes considerable knowledge, courage and will just to follow one's direction fully, as it not only means inventing methods and constraint yourself, but also because of the sheer difficulty of having to resist the temptation of resigning to previous expected standards as an individual.

That, and the lack of understanding between you and others witnessing you persue what you want, as well. After all, [[Communication is the cornerstone of evolution]].

Doing what you want will lead to more thoughts, meaningful thoughts, and understanding of the world, and moreover, what  you actually want. But I wouldn't go as far as to say it will be a pleasant experience. That said, it could also be very rewarding, as well, so one should expect a variety of things to brace themselves for the journey ahead of them.
