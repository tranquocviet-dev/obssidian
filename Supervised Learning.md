Supervised Learning is the type of [[Machine Learning]] that feeds on filtered, processed data that aims to Categorize the data given. It is a study of how the machine looks at the data and categorize them. 

The end goal of Supervised Learning is be able to make predictions with relative accuracy. It can take the form of Classification (surrownding the data, seperating data) or Regression (drawing a line).

Classification is the act of seperating data and putting them into different places. Multiple types of them exist: binary (two types only), multi-class (one to one with many classes), multi-label (one to many with many labels).
