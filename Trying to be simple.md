I feel like [[A lot of people underestimate the difficluty of being simple]]. Me included, at the beginning, I found myself hating the idea of complexity, simply because it was unecessary. [[Complexity is sometimes good]], though. Meeting [[Tran Huy Dang]] made me realize just how much I have missed the actual fun of being caring towards life, towards others, and especially how my stuff looks.

[[Simplicity does not mean good]]. I had to learn it the hard way when i try to be simple, and moreover, I realized that when [[I try to make things unique]], it often comes back to bite me. Sometimes not, but [[Everytime I try I learn new things]], so it was not all bad.
